School Teacher Awareness Program

BIT Durg AICTE IDEA LAB has organized a week-long Teachers Awareness program which started from 20th June aimed for school teachers to learn and develop the production, manufacturing and making of projects. The skilling programme is part of All India Institute of Technical Education IDEA LAB scheme intended to encourage product development and research publication in the country. The Idea Lab in charges Dr Anil Kumar, Dr Santosh Mishra, Mrs. Suchitra Pandey and Mr. Kauleshwar Prasad, along with their team of student ambassadors provided training in the field of Laser Cutting & CNC Machine, 3D Scanning & 3D Printing, Design and Fabrication of Printed Circuit Board, Design and Development of IOT and Machine Learning Based Product, Arduino Programming. The purpose of IDEA Lab is to provide all facilities under one roof, for conversion of an idea into a prototype. With these facilities in the campus, the school teachers were encouraged to take up the process, got training and developed skills like creative thinking, problem-solving, decision-making collaboration etc. 30 teachers from different schools around Bhilai-Durg participated in this awareness program. Both theoretical as well as practical training of the foresaid techniques were provided during the course of this awareness program. Dr. Arun Arora (Chief Mentor, BIT AICTE IDEA LAB), Dr. Mohan Kumar Gupta (Coordinator, BIT AICTE IDEA LAB) and Dr. Pawan Kumar Patnaik (Co-coordinator BIT AICTE IDEA LAB) marked their presence at the inaugural session. Dr. Arun Arora said “This IDEA Lab initiative will transform conventional classroom teaching to practical oriented teaching. According to new education policy this type of program is very fruitful for school teachers as well as students”. Dr. Mohan Kumar Gupta said “We welcome all the teachers from school fraternity to utilize the facilities available at our IDEA LAB.” Dr. Pawan Kumar Patnaik delivered a session on IDEA Lab Scheme, Initiatives and its functioning. Feedback from the Teachers: The school teachers felt very enthusiastic to learn that the advanced facilities have been established in our institute which will provide practical learning experience in the field of smart and intelligent manufacturing. The feedback form was circulated among the teachers and few words from the participants are: “Best place for research”, “Workshop should be there for two weeks, and it should be there for students also”, “More such training programs for teachers as well as for students should be conducted frequently.”, “Program was awesome”, “Kindly arrange workshop for students also for more than one week.”
